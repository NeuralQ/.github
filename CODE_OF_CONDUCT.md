# Code of Conduct

NeuralQ is committed to maintaining a highly professional, secure, and collaborative environment for all employees, contractors, and partners interacting with our codebase and infrastructure.

## Professional Standards

We expect all contributors to adhere to the following standards:
* **Excellence and Rigor:** Engage in thorough, objective, and constructive code reviews.
* **Respectful Communication:** Maintain a professional tone in all pull requests, issues, and internal communications.
* **Security First:** Prioritize the security of our intellectual property and client data above all else.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors will not be tolerated:
* Harassment, discrimination, or unprofessional conduct of any kind.
* Sharing, exposing, or mishandling proprietary code, trade secrets, or client data.
* Disregarding established security protocols and engineering guidelines.

## Enforcement

Violations of this code of conduct, particularly those regarding security and intellectual property, will result in immediate revocation of repository access and may lead to termination of contract and legal action. 

To report a professional grievance, please contact leadership directly at [hello@neuralq.ai](mailto:hello@neuralq.ai).
