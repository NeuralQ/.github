# Security Policy

NeuralQ takes the security of our artificial intelligence architectures, quantum-inspired algorithms, and client data infrastructure with the utmost seriousness. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of security and confidentiality.

## Supported Versions

As a deep-tech consulting firm developing proprietary and bespoke solutions, security updates and patches are provided exclusively for active client deployments and our current internal production systems. 

| Version / Deployment | Supported |
| :--- | :--- |
| Active Client Deployments | Yes |
| Current Internal Production | Yes |
| Legacy / Deprecated Prototypes | No |

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you believe you have discovered a potential security vulnerability in any NeuralQ software, infrastructure, or machine learning model, please report it to us immediately. 

**Do not open a public issue or publicly disclose the vulnerability.** 

Given the proprietary nature of our technology, we require all vulnerability reports to be kept strictly confidential.

1. **Email your report** to [security@neuralq.ai](mailto:security@neuralq.ai).
2. **Include detailed information**, such as:
   * A description of the vulnerability.
   * Steps to reproduce the issue.
   * The specific system, model, or codebase affected.
   * Potential impact or risk assessment.

## Response Process

* We will acknowledge receipt of your vulnerability report within 48 hours.
* Our engineering team will triage and investigate the issue confidentially.
* We will provide updates on our progress and notify you when a patch or mitigation has been deployed.

## Responsible Disclosure

We ask that you act in good faith to protect the privacy and data of NeuralQ and our clients. Please do not exploit the vulnerability beyond what is absolutely necessary to verify its existence, and do not share details of the vulnerability with any third parties without our explicit written permission.
